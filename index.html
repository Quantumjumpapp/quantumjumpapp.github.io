Theta: 3-8 hertz

Theta brainwaves occur during the transition between sleep and wakefulness, characterized by free-flowing thoughts, creativity and intuition.

Alpha: 8-12 hertz

Alpha brainwaves are present when the mind is at rest, such as during yoga or meditation, resulting in a relaxed physical and mental state.

Gamma: 25-100 hertz

The highest frequency of the brainwaves, Gamma brainwaves are present during heightened awareness and expanded consciousness.
